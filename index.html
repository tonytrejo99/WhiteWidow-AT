WhiteWidow

In Speed We Trust



WhiteWidow is a 1990 Chevy Silverado single cab with a 400SS clone body conversion. Originally a V6 pickup, WhiteWidow got a 5.7L 350 V8 swapped with a five speed manual transmission. Saddly, WhiteWidow only has a K&N Intake with Flowmasters Exhaust. Sitting on 15X8 in the font and 15X10 in the rear, WhiteWidow weights aproxx 3800 lbs. A freshly new pearl white paint job with red bedliner, WhiteWidow has the attention of all cops in the area.

  • Top speed - 125 mph
